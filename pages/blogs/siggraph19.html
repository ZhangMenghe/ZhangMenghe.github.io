  • FaceUnity

    • Runs on server(feature capture/style transfer etc)
  • Neural AR(by Akaatsuki)
    • Immersive augmented reality with real-time neural style transfer.
    • Interaction: third-party controller
    • Computation: backpack computer(HP)
    • Display: Google cardboard
    • Style transfer is so coooool in AR
  • OVS+Tumor
    • A Tool for Enhanced Lung Tumor Annotation in VR for Machine Learning Training and Analysis.

    • high quality rendered, wireless vr

    • Immersive Mixed Reality System using Video-See-Thru HMD and 3D LiDAR scanne
    • LIDAR BASED SPATIAL MAPPING. Use lidar to reconstruc environment
